Services for Receptions

Accomodations

Reserve a block of rooms for your guests at special rates at our hotel with plenty of time before the big event. At most hotels, and as a general rule, the minimum number of guests rooms required to make a block is 10 rooms per night. If, however, you are holding the reception at our hotel or using any banquet space, that number can be flexed. This way your guests can have a choice between different locations and prices. (TempleInnAndSuites.com).

Food Service

Our wedding catering service specializes in Asian cuisine including: Chinese, Japanese, Korean, and fusion food. We offer a 10 course sit down style or buffet style catering to meet your needs for your event. Our menus are separated by "Sit Down Style" and "Buffet Style" and feature a wide selection of food. Choose from one of our delicious menus to create an unforgettable event to satisfy and impress your guests. We will bring our catering services to you for your special day.

Setup

Choose a package for stress free planning or create a unique floor plan, special table design - anything you’d like to make your own. Our wedding professionals will make that pretty stress free too. With the essentials taken care of, you're free to customize from there. Dining tables, chairs, linens (choice of white or ivory), table settings, and all standard set-up charges; Head table, chairs, and linen; Skirted tables for such items as your cake, DJ, name cards, guest book, and gifts; Dance floor; Taste test for two.
