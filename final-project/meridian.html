History
The Meridian Idaho Temple is a temple of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints (LDS Church) in Meridian, Idaho. The intent to build the temple was announced by church president Thomas S. Monson on April 4, 2011, during the church's semi-annual general conference. The temple was dedicated on November 19, 2017 by Dieter F. Uchtdorf.
